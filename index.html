About Modified Nationals

The UK’s biggest celebration of modified culture, performance builds, and automotive creativity.

Modified Nationals brings together thousands of enthusiasts for a full‑throttle weekend of cars, community, and culture. From high‑powered track weapons to meticulously detailed show cars, this is where the scene comes alive.

Built for the Scene

Every corner of the event is shaped by passion — the builders, the clubs, the traders, the photographers, and the fans who make this community what it is. Whether you’re showcasing your pride and joy or exploring the latest trends, you’ll find your place here.

Be Part of the Action

Immerse yourself in the atmosphere, connect with fellow enthusiasts, and experience a weekend that celebrates the culture behind the cars. Modified Nationals isn’t just an event — it’s a movement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I camp at the event?

Yes — weekend camping is available with pre-booked tickets.

How do I enter my car?

Use the Show Car Application Form on the Show Cars page.

Are dogs allowed?

Yes, but they must be kept on a lead at all times.
